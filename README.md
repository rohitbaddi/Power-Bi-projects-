🔎 Overview
This repository showcases a collection of interactive Power BI dashboards designed to deliver actionable insights across business performance, HR analytics, and sales operations. Each dashboard combines advanced data modeling with intuitive visualization to highlight trends, KPIs, and decision‑support metrics.

The goal of these dashboards is to transform raw data into strategic intelligence—empowering stakeholders to monitor performance, identify opportunities, and make informed decisions.

📈 Dashboard Explanations
1. Business Performance Dashboard
Purpose: Tracks company‑wide KPIs including sales, orders, profit, and shipping costs.

Highlights:

Sales vs Target achievement (+2.84% above goal).

Order volume vs Target (+6.26% above goal).

Profitability breakdown by region, category, and sub‑category.

Insights into order priority profitability (Medium priority orders drive the highest profit).

Trend analysis of profit vs sales quantity across months.

Value: Enables leadership to identify profitable regions, optimize shipping costs, and prioritize categories driving growth.

2. HR Analytics Dashboard
Purpose: Provides a holistic view of workforce composition, salary distribution, and leave management.

Highlights:

Average salary: $64,676, employee count: 161, average age: 35.22 years.

Gender distribution: Male 54.66% vs Female 45.34%.

Job role segmentation across Packaging, Production, Sales, Research, and Marketing.

Leave balance analysis by job title, highlighting areas exceeding targets.

Headcount growth trend from 2017–2023.

Qualification vs salary scatter plot for workforce skill benchmarking.

Value: Supports HR teams in monitoring diversity, workforce planning, and leave policy optimization.

3. Employee Demographics Dashboard
Purpose: Focuses on workforce demographics and hiring trends for a chocolate manufacturing company.

Highlights:

Average salary: $54K, total leave balance: 2K, employees with >20 days leave: 109.

Gender split: 54% Female, 46% Male.

Age distribution by gender across bins (20–60 years).

Hiring trend analysis showing cumulative employee growth from 2017–2023.

Salary vs educational qualification scatter plot (Bachelor’s, Diploma, Master’s).

Value: Helps HR and management understand workforce diversity, salary equity, and hiring patterns.

4. Classic Car Company Sales Dashboard
Purpose: Analyzes global sales performance for a classic car company.

Highlights:

Total Sales: ₹9.61M, Quantity Sold: 105.53K, Profit: ₹3.83M.

Target achievement: ₹5.52M vs ₹1.77M goal (+212%).

Category‑wise contribution: Classic Cars dominate with ₹3.85M sales.

Top 5 products (e.g., Ferrari 360 Spider, Harley‑Davidson Eagle Drag Bike).

Bottom 5 products highlighting underperformers.

Global sales distribution across North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia.

Value: Enables product managers to identify bestsellers, optimize inventory, and focus on high‑margin categories.

5. Profitability Insights Dashboard
Purpose: Provides a deep dive into profitability across categories, regions, and order priorities.

Highlights:

Profit vs Target achievement: $881.9K earned.

Segment‑wise shipping cost analysis (Corporate segment highest at 51.96%).

Regional profitability: Europe and Asia Pacific lead.

Category profitability: Technology dominates with ~$0.5M profit.

Sub‑category profitability: Copiers and Phones drive the highest margins.

Monthly profit trend analysis for operational forecasting.
